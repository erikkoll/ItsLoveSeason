Our Story

It all began on a warm summer afternoon in Barcelona. The temperature was perfect and the city was alive with energy. But that's not where the main characters were. One was in rainy London, and the other had woken up at 3am in pretty Flower Mound, Texas. And they were both, (un)fortunately, on Microsoft Teams.

It took them until spring 2021 to meet in person, when both relocated to London. They did their first trip together (as friends) on the last day of August to the coastal town of Hastings. They were having fish & chips and chilling in the grass, unaware that millennia ago some important events took place that would shaped the course of the country’s history: Battle of Hastings.

Over two years, their friendship grew as they spent more time together. It wasn’t until a trip to Italy with friends in April 2022 that they realised they might be interested in more. This realisation may have been aided by a night in a club and a bottle of strong Sambuca…

That summer, they finally started dating! They had one of their first dates at a rooftop cinema in London.

In October 2022, an opportunity to move in together came when one of Gabriela's flatmates moved out. They decided to take it and started living together in a Clapham flat with friends Marco and Phoebe.

When it was time to find their own place one year later, they moved into a cozy flat with a garden just 10 minutes down the road and within a quick walk of several friends' places.They still live in that same flat and love the community they’ve built!

They have spent the last few years traveling, eating, picking up new hobbies, and continuing to work in London as sustainability and social impact consultants. They love hosting friends for Autumn festivities, and Eric has even gotten Gabriela to do a couple of triathlons. (See the photos at the end for some highlights!)

On the 4th of May 2025, Eric proposed to Gabriela by a lake, in the city of Voss, Norway.

On the 4th of September 2025, they were joined by their parents and siblings for a small civil ceremony in London, United Kingdom.

We’re so excited to have you all join us for our next big milestone – a celebration for our "official" wedding in Spain!

“From the first moment I saw your sleepy face at 3am in a Teams call, I knew I would ask for a retainer on your consultancy services for life.”
– Eric

“It took me a few shots of very strong Italian alcohol to realise that no one in a 3-meter radius could run a 5k faster than you.”
– Gabriela
